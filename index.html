Welcometo Craftverse

Craftverse is an online community for cosplayers to share, learn, and connect. It’s a first of its kind community for cosplayers and crafters to exchange secrets, make a name for themselves, and explore business opportunities.

Engage in the community

Our mission at Craftverse is to give cosplay professionals and enthusiasts alike a space to showcase their craft, inspire, explore lucrative opportunities, and unlock their potential ina safe industry-friendly environment.

Express yourself

CraftVerse is a home for cosplay enthusiasts. Gone are the days of fragmented online cosplay groups. Together we will create a welcoming environment where you can share your latest creations and projects.

Grow Your Knowledge

What’s your goal? Blacksmithing? 3D Printing? Painting? From sowing to foam work, we’ll work with industry leaders to help you learn the latest cosplay secrets and techniques.

Have Fun
Make Money.

Craftverse will empower you to sell digital and physical products to the worldwide cosplay community. Together we will connect, makers, brands, and events to the vast world of cosplay and crafting.
